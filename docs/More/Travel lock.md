The travel lock function is useful when want to avoid any buttons accidentally being pressed and thereby changing settings.

Helpful for traveling and storing the clock for longer periods.

## How to lock
Go to "More" > "Display settings" > "Travel lock". 

## How to unlock
In travel lock mode the display will either read "Press and hold" or "Connect to power". 
To unlock please connect to USB power, wait for the display to read "Press and hold", and then press the selector wheel button for more than 5 seconds. 

When the display shows a time format you may release the selector button.
